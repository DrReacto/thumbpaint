Neveramin — Support

Optical theremin. No antenna, no touching, never.

Have a question, found a bug, or something not working right? Email fluxaidigital@gmail.com — include your device model and iOS version if it's a bug, and it'll get looked at.

Getting started

Press the NEVERAMIN button (the round red button, or the rectangular button above the camera view on iPhone). Grant camera access when asked. Keep your hands out of frame for a few seconds while it calibrates — then wave a hand in front of the camera: moving left and right bends the pitch, moving closer to the camera raises the volume.

Frequently asked questions

The screen says "CALIBRATING" and then nothing happens.
Calibration needs your hands completely out of frame for it to photograph the empty background. Once it finishes (status text disappears), wave a hand into view — the app only makes sound once it detects motion.

I don't hear anything.
Check that your device isn't in silent/mute mode and the volume is turned up. Also make sure a hand is actually moving in the camera's view — Neveramin is silent until it detects motion.

It asked for camera access and I said no by mistake.
Go to Settings → Neveramin → and enable Camera access, then relaunch the app.

The picture looks wrong / tracking feels off.
Try restarting the calibration by pressing the NEVERAMIN button again in a room with steady, even lighting — flickering lights or a lot of background motion (like a TV in frame) can confuse the tracker.

Does it work without an internet connection?
Yes — Neveramin makes no network requests at all, so it works identically with Wi-Fi, cellular, or fully offline.
