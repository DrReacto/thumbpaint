Neveramin — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Short version: Neveramin doesn't collect, store, or transmit any data about you, because it doesn't need to. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

What data we collect

None. Neveramin has no account system, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting, and no backend server of any kind. The app makes no network requests while you use it.

Camera access

Neveramin uses your device's camera to track hand movement, which controls the pitch and volume of the instrument. This video is processed live, entirely on your device, purely as pixel brightness comparisons frame-to-frame — it is never recorded, saved, uploaded, or shared with anyone, including us. Nothing from the camera ever leaves your device.

Microphone access

Your device's operating system may request microphone permission alongside camera permission as a bundled system prompt. Neveramin does not use or access microphone audio at all — the app only produces sound, it never listens.

Third parties

Neveramin does not integrate with any third-party services, does not use cookies or tracking technologies, and does not share information with anyone because it doesn't collect any in the first place.

Children's privacy

Neveramin does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, since it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a revised date above.
