P23 AV Presenter — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Short version: P23 AV Presenter doesn't collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. Everything the app does — importing media, playing it, and sending it to an external screen — happens entirely on your device.

What data we collect

None. P23 has no account system, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no crash reporting, and no backend server of any kind. We have no ability to see who you are or what you do in the app.

Your media and settings

The media you import or capture (video, images, audio, and PDF slide decks) and your pad setups and settings are stored only in the app's local storage on your device. This content is never uploaded, sent to us, or shared with anyone. Removing the app removes this data.

Camera access

P23 uses your device's camera for its live external-input feature (showing a camera feed on the output) and, when you choose to, for capturing a photo or video into your library. Camera video is processed and stored entirely on your device. It is never recorded to any server, uploaded, or shared with anyone, including us.

Microphone access

Your device's operating system may bundle a microphone permission with the camera permission. P23 only uses the microphone when you deliberately record a video with the camera, and that audio stays on your device as part of the recorded clip. The app does not otherwise listen to or record audio.

Photo Library access

When you import media, P23 accesses only the specific photos or videos you select. Nothing else in your library is read, and nothing is uploaded or shared.

Purchases

The optional one-time "P23 Pro" upgrade is processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles the transaction; P23 never receives your name, Apple ID, or payment details. Please see Apple's privacy policy for how Apple handles purchases.

Third parties

P23 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services, and does not use cookies or similar technologies. There is no information to share, because none is collected.

Children's privacy

P23 does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, since it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a revised date above.
