P23 AV Presenter — Support

Live audio-visual playback from a pad grid, on your iPad.

Have a question, found a bug, or something not working right? Email mrchrisn@gmail.com — include your iPad model and iPadOS version if it's a bug, and it'll get looked at.

Getting started

Tap IMPORT / LIBRARY to bring in your media (from Photos, Files, or the camera). Turn on PAD PARAM and tap an empty pad to assign a file to it. Turn PAD PARAM off, then tap a pad to play it instantly. Use GO to step forward through your assigned pads in order, BACK to step back, and STOP to clear the output (it remembers your place so GO resumes).

Frequently asked questions

What files can I import?
Video (H.264 MP4/MOV), images (JPG/PNG), audio (WAV/AIFF/MP3), and PDF slide decks. Import from your Photo Library or the Files app, or capture a photo/video with the camera.

How do I show a PowerPoint or Keynote deck?
Export it as a PDF (both PowerPoint and Keynote have "Export as PDF"), then import the PDF and assign it to a pad. Selecting the pad opens page 1; GO advances one page at a time and BACK goes back a page. This keeps your slides pixel-exact and works fully offline.

How do I send the show to a projector or TV?
Connect an HDMI adapter to the iPad. P23 automatically sends a clean program feed — picture and sound — to the external screen, while the iPad keeps the controls. Use the official Apple USB-C or Lightning AV adapter with its charger connected for the most reliable results. If you have Screen Mirroring turned on in Control Center, turn it off so the app uses the wired HDMI output.

A video imported but plays black, or won't import.
P23 plays H.264 video. If a clip comes in black or is rejected, re-export or convert it to H.264 MP4 and import it again. Importing from your Photo Library usually converts to a compatible format automatically.

It asked for camera access and I said no by mistake.
Go to Settings → P23 AV Presenter → and enable Camera access, then relaunch the app. The camera is used for the live external-input feature (EXT SRC) and for capturing photos/videos into your library.

What's in the free version, and what does the upgrade add?
The free version is fully usable: a working pad bank, all media types including PDF slide decks, fades, slide shows, and HDMI output. The one-time P23 Pro in-app purchase unlocks the complete rig — all four banks (hundreds of pads), macros, save/load shows, SPEED and scratch control, COLOR grading, reverse, and strobe.

I bought Pro but it's not unlocked (new device, reinstall, etc.).
Open the upgrade screen and tap Restore purchase. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in to it, including via Family Sharing.

Does it work without an internet connection?
Yes — P23 runs entirely on your device and needs no internet to import, play, or output your show. (An internet connection is only needed the first time to download the app and to make or restore a purchase, both handled by the App Store.)
